Fast and Easy Chicken & Rice

The best thing about this recipe is that it takes minutes to prepare and only takes a few minutes to heat up in the microwave.

Ingredients:

  • Trader Joe's Frozen Grilled Chicken
  • Trader Joes's Frozen bagged rice
  • Vegatable of choice
  • Soyaki

Steps:

  1. Combine frozen rice, chicken and vegatable of choice in microwave safe dish.
  2. Lightly drizzle Trader Joe's Soyaki.
  3. Put in freezer for a quick and easy meal for work.
  4. Enjoy!
