Caprese Salad

The POWERof cheese!

Kale typically is hard to muscle down. With the help of cheese and few other ingredients... Kale can be a part of anyone diet.

Ingredients:

  • Kale (preferablly dino)
  • Lemons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Shred kale as fine as desired.
  2. Place in mixing bowl and grate 1 cup of parmasan cheese.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Cut lemon in half and squeeze half of lemon on salad.
  5. Finally, lightly drizzle extra-virgin olive oil and give one last stir.
  6. Enjoy!
